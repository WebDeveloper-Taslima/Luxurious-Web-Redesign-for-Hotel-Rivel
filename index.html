The Rivel Difference

As a non-profit retreat founded by Costa Ricans, we offer an authentic luxury experience that gives back to our community. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of our exclusive lodges, farm-to-table cuisine, and curated nature experiences—all designed to rejuvenate your spirit.

Our commitment to sustainability and local culture creates a truly immersive experience that connects you with the heart of Costa Rica while providing world-class luxury and service.
